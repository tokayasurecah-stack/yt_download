Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how YT Downloader ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the desktop application or visit this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The YT Downloader desktop application does not:

  • Collect or transmit personal data to any server
  • Track URLs you download
  • Store usage statistics or analytics
  • Require account registration
  • Access files outside your chosen download folder

2. Internet Connections Made by the App

The App connects to the internet solely for the following purposes:

  • Fetching the YouTube video/audio you explicitly request
  • Loading advertisement banner images (if configured)
  • Checking for application updates (future feature)

No personal information is included in these requests beyond what is technically required (e.g., your IP address as part of normal internet communication).

3. Third-Party Services

This website may display advertisements served by Google AdSense or similar third-party ad networks. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to show relevant ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

The App uses yt-dlp (an open-source library) to fetch media from YouTube. Please review YouTube's Terms of Service.

4. Cookies

The desktop application does not use cookies. This website may use essential cookies for analytics and advertising purposes in accordance with applicable law.

5. Children's Privacy

YT Downloader is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via GitHub Issues.
